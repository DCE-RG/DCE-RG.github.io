Overview

This is a weakly reading group of Data-Centric Engineering (DCE) group in The Alan Turing Institute. The reading / talk topics is broad from theoretical statistics to statistical methodology. The topics will be changed rouhgly by month. Regular time is 11:00am - 12:00am on every Wednesday (it may be changed depends on week). Please see our schedule below to check detail of topics and time for the weekly group. This group is open to everyone. If you would like to join the group and to assign to our emailing list, please feel free to contact organizers.
