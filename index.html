Overview

This is a weakly reading group of Data-Centric Engineering (DCE) group in The Alan Turing Institute (ATI). The reading & talk topic is broad from statistical theory, methodology, and application (e.g. stocastic process, optimization, surrogate modelling, etc.). Regular time is 11:00am - 12:00am on every Wednesday but it may be changed depends on speakers. Please see our schedule below to check detail of reading & talk topics and time. This group is open to everyone. If you would like to join the group and the emailing list, please feel free to contact organizers.
